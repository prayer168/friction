【繁體中文】
摩擦力不是越大越好，也不只是「阻礙運動」。我們能走路、煞車和抓穩物品，都少不了接觸面之間的摩擦力。

《摩擦力搜查隊》是一份適合國小高年級的互動數位教材。實驗室現在有兩個場景：在「平面推箱」中調整表面、質量與推力；在「斜坡滑幣」中比較 5°–45° 的角度與三種粗糙度，先預測錢幣會不會滑動、會滑多遠，再用力與距離的數據修正想法。

孩子可以保存六次實驗紀錄，還能透過五階段動畫、八個生活案例與十題闖關，逐步理解靜摩擦力、動摩擦力、臨界角與啟動門檻。

【English】
Friction is not simply “the bigger, the better,” and it does more than slow things down. Walking, braking, and holding objects securely all depend on friction between contacting surfaces.

Friction Force Detectives is an interactive science lesson for upper-elementary learners with two laboratory scenes. In “Push the Box,” students change the surface, mass, and applied force. In “Coin on a Ramp,” they compare angles from 5° to 45° and three surface textures, predict whether the coin will move and how far it will travel, then revise their ideas using force and distance data.

Students can save six trials and continue with a five-stage animation, eight real-life cases, and a ten-question challenge to explore static friction, kinetic friction, critical angles, and the threshold for motion.
