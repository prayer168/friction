【繁體中文】
摩擦力不是越大越好，也不只是「阻礙運動」。我們能走路、煞車和抓穩物品，都少不了接觸面之間的摩擦力。

《摩擦力搜查隊》是一份適合國小高年級的互動數位教材。孩子可以調整表面、箱子質量與推力，先預測再測試，記錄六次實驗證據；還能透過五階段動畫、八個生活案例與十題闖關，理解靜摩擦力、動摩擦力和啟動門檻。

【English】
Friction is not simply “the bigger, the better,” and it does more than slow things down. Walking, braking, and holding objects securely all depend on friction between contacting surfaces.

Friction Force Detectives is an interactive science lesson for upper-elementary learners. Students change the surface, box mass, and applied force; make a prediction before testing; and record evidence from up to six trials. A five-stage animation, eight real-life cases, and a ten-question challenge help them distinguish static and kinetic friction and understand the threshold for motion.
